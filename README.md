### About Me

I am a computational mechanics researcher specialising in earthquake engineering, with a focus on nonlinear seismic soil-structure interaction, solid FEM modelling, and constitutive model development. I obtained my Ph.D. from IUSS Pavia (2024), where I investigated 3D numerical modelling of seismic SSI effects on masonry towers.

My day-to-day work involves writing C++ material models and element formulations for [OpenSees](https://opensees.berkeley.edu/), running large-scale nonlinear FE simulations, and developing Python tools for pre/post-processing seismic analyses.

### Research Interests

- 3D nonlinear seismic soil-structure interaction
- Solid FEM modelling in OpenSees (C++ development)
- Cyclic constitutive modelling of soils
- Implicit-explicit numerical integration schemes
- Seismic analysis of shallow-founded masonry structures
